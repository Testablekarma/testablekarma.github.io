Privacy Summary:

This Privacy Policy describes how Mark Webb ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Auread mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • User Preferences: Settings and preferences you configure within the App.

Note: The App does not require you to create an account. All data is stored locally on your device.

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, including features accessed, frequency of use, and performance data.
  • Log Data: IP address, app crashes, system activity, and the date and time of your requests.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Firebase: We collect anonymized analytics and app performance data through Firebase to improve app functionality and user experience.
  • Sentry: We collect error and crash reports through Sentry to improve app stability and fix technical issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Services: Deliver the App's functionality and features.
  • Maintain and Improve: Operate, maintain, and improve the App's functionality and user experience.
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide technical support.
  • Analytics: Analyze usage patterns to improve our services and develop new features.
  • Security: Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security concerns.
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal content on our servers.

3.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL protocols

  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure deletion of data upon request

3.3 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services or as required by law. You can delete your content and app data at any time through the App settings or by uninstalling the App.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We share data with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including:

  • Firebase (Google): For analytics, performance monitoring, and understanding usage patterns (with anonymized data)

These services only receive anonymized usage and diagnostic data. Your personal content remains on your device.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations or court orders

4.3 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5.1 Access and Correction

You can access and update your preferences within the App settings at any time. Your content is stored locally on your device.
